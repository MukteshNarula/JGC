FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What is Jolly Good Chef?

Jolly Good Chef is an AI-powered cooking platform that generates personalised recipes based on your preferences, dietary requirements, and available ingredients. It also helps you plan your weekly meals and creates smart grocery lists.

Is Jolly Good Chef free to use?

Yes! Jolly Good Chef offers a free plan with basic recipe generation (5 recipes/month). Premium plans (₹99/month or ₹999/year) unlock unlimited recipes, advanced meal planning, and smart grocery lists.

How does the AI recipe generator work?

Simply tell the AI your dietary preferences, the ingredients you have, cuisine type, and serving size. Our AI engine generates a complete, step-by-step recipe tailored specifically to your needs in seconds.

Can I get a grocery list from my meal plan?

Absolutely! Once you've planned your meals for the week, Jolly Good Chef automatically compiles a consolidated, optimised grocery list so you never miss an ingredient.

What cuisines does Jolly Good Chef support?

Jolly Good Chef supports a wide range of cuisines including Indian, Italian, Chinese, Mediterranean, Mexican, and many more. You can also specify regional Indian cuisines like Punjabi, South Indian, Bengali, and others.

Can I cancel my premium subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. Your premium access will remain active until the end of your current billing period.
